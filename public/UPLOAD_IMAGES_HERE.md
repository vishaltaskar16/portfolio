## How to Upload

1. Place all your images in the `/public/images/` folder
2. Make sure the filenames match exactly (case-sensitive)
3. The images will automatically appear in your portfolio

## Image Optimization Tips

- **Compress your images** to reduce file size (use tools like TinyPNG)
- **Use descriptive content** that showcases your work
- **Ensure good quality** - avoid blurry or pixelated images
- **Keep file sizes under 500KB** for faster loading
